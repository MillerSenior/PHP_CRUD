To display php files: 
-In url: http://www.srv.php 
-Go to: http://www.srv.php/filename.php
-Or go to: http://www.srv.php/foldername/filename.php
-Make sure all files are in the Home Directory/public_html folder
(all files must be called from browser using filepaths that originate in your public_html (PHP) environment folder)
